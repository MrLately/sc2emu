# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and technical collaboration environment.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be respectful and professional in technical disagreement.
- Assume good intent and focus on evidence.
- Give actionable feedback.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Doxxing, threats, or intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or ban contributors for behavior that violates this code.

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and related project communication channels.
